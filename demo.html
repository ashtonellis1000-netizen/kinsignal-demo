What Breaks Today

The first 30 minutes are where injury response falls apart.

Not from negligence. From chaos, no clear system, and tools that weren't built for the supervisor standing at the scene.

📵
Emergency contacts are buried.

Paper binders at the front office. HR systems that need a desktop login. The supervisor has a phone and no number — while the clock is running.

🧠
Supervisors document from memory.

The incident report gets written hours or days later. Timestamps are guesses. Witness accounts have shifted. That's the record if the case is ever disputed.

⏰
OSHA reporting risk is unclear.

Fatalities: 8-hour reporting window. In-patient hospitalizations, amputations, eye loss: 24 hours. Most front-line supervisors have never been told these rules exist.

📎
Witness notes and photos get scattered.

Someone's personal phone. A sticky note. An email thread. When HR or legal asks for everything six months later, nobody can reconstruct it cleanly.

How KinSignal Works

Six steps. One guided flow. Nothing missed.

KinSignal is a step-by-step workflow built for the supervisor at the scene — not a form to fill out later from memory.
